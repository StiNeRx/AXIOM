The Solution: Recursive Spiral Curriculum

Current education teaches subjects in silos. AXIOM introduces high-level realities immediately, then deconstructs them over time. We harness the neuroplasticity of ages 4-7 to build intuition before formalism.

Ages 4-7

Phase I: The Intuition

Visuospatial & Tactile. No textbooks. Direct exposure to anomalies.

EXAMPLE: GRAVITY Rolling marbles on a stretched fabric sheet. Goal: "See" geometry.
Ages 8-12

Phase II: The Syntax

Deconstruction. Giving names to phenomena. Introduction of Logic.

EXAMPLE: GRAVITY Explaining "Geodesics" & Vectors using graphs. Goal: Define the path.
Ages 13-16

Phase III: The Rigor

Formalization. Deriving standard formulas from intuition.

EXAMPLE: GRAVITY Einstein Field Equations. $G_{\mu\nu} = T_{\mu\nu}$ Goal: Calculate the system.
